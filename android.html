SoundMix APP

Experience music the way it was meant to be

UI Design
Created with Figma
Duration: 6 weeks

Approach

I did a thorough research on some music apps to know and find out what is missing and what could be added to attract more users

Problem

I found out that most music app cannot store music to play offline when they are not conneted to the internet and also they do not have more suggestions for similar music when an artist is selected

Solution

I decided to add a download button for users to be able to download and store music in SoundMix and added more suggestions for any particular music selected to play
