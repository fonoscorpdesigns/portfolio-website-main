Pocket Vault

UI/UX Design case study
Created with Figma
Duration: 6 weeks

Project Brief

I did a thorough research on some music apps to know and find out what is missing and what could be added to attract more users

The 5Ws

Where?
Saving money can be really hard, especially when you don’t have a long time to do so

Who?
This tool is for people who want to save money, quickly, for a particular reason

When?
Users will utilize the tool in the period before a planned expense

What?
A responsive website, web app, and/or mobile app allowing all data on income and expenses to be recorded easily

Why?
This tool is for people who want to save money, quickly, for a particular reason

Guiding Principles

Safe
Get secured with Pocket Vault, avoid unneccessary losses, unaccounted spending and get every accountability of each cent

Trust
What we sell is trust as we build your economical lifestyle with the help of our financial management

Reliable
Your reliablility with Pocket Vault strengthens the actiualization of your dreams

Commitment
Being committed accounts for drastic change that takes you to your heart desires

Confident
By selling our trust to you, your reliablity and commitment with us, a total confidence is achieved

I decided to design a simple app that will give your target users a way of saving and managing their expenses as they go about their normal daily life

I want your target users to feel safe why using this app and make referrals to other people

You can be able to build their trust and your target users can stick to your financial managing team’s advice when prompted

Making Pocket Vault reliable with simple explanations, direct guidelines and giving your target users full support when needed without complain

A user should be confident at the end of budget, savings and expenses cycle peroid and be sure of a constant usage of Pocket Vault

Have your target users make full commitment to the financial managing plans for Pocket Vault to work

Tweaks

  • I changed the slide navigations in the 3 welcoming pages to a standard navigation which users are user friendly with
  • I added a Register link to the Login Screen
  • Specified the income and expense categories in a different page for clarification
  • Made sure all final input shows on the record page
  • Created a button for EDIT, DELETE and INFO to Edit a record, Delete a record or navigate to the Analysis page through the Info button
  • Created a popup overlay to SET amount or DELETE record
  • A user can be able to start a new process by navigating to the HOME screen to start a new process inputting income and expenses
