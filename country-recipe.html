Country Recipe

UI/UX Design Case Study
Created with Figma
Duration: 9 weeks

About Country Recipe

A cooking app which gives users the opportunity to narrow their search by selecting the country they want to find a recipe from. Users will have the opportunity to get the missing ingredients needed to add to the ingredients they already have, the complete ingredients they need to make recipe(s) and also allow users to add their special recipe to the app

Design Approach

Brief and early ideation research

I browsed through a lot of cooking apps and found out that most cooking apps are the same, showing different recipes and ingredients needed, no room to add a favorite recipe, introducing notes and videos for learning and even having your daily weekly meal planner.

What I gathered

All these apps display the ingredients and recipe in general for viewers to see, but there is no room for users selecting the country they are from or the country they want to try their recipe. Also these app only allow users to add favorites and save recipes but they do not have section for users to add their own contribution of their special recipe for other users to try

Conducting a Usability Testing
I decided it is time I go for a usability test to put me in the right direction. From my experience, it is better to have your designs tested at the initial stage which is less stressful, avoid major errors and saves energy to start all over again. I reached out to friends and fellow designers to book an appointment with them for a usability testing
